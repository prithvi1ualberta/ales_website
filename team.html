ALES GSA Advisor

Dr. Guillermo Hernandez Ramirez

Associate Dean of Graduate & Postdoctoral Studies

Guillermo has been working at University of Alberta since 2012. He is currently a professor and gladly serves as associate dean of graduate & postdoctoral studies at Faculty of ALES. He has previously lived and studied or worked in countries such as New Zealand, Costa Rica, Panama, Germany, Ecuador, Venezuela, and United States. Interacting with graduate students in ALES is insightful and rewarding for Guillermo as this work underpins impactful education and their future careers.
